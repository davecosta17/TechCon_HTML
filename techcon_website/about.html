Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a small meetup for developers in Accra. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for innovation, attracting thousands of attendees from across Africa and beyond. From humble beginnings to a major tech movement, TechCon has always been about community, creativity, and progress.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to empower the next generation of tech leaders by fostering collaboration, showcasing emerging technologies, and driving conversations that matter. We believe in inclusive innovation and the power of technology to transform lives.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ama Asantewaa

Renowned AI researcher and advocate for ethical machine learning. Her 2022 keynote on “Bias in Algorithms” sparked global conversations on fairness in tech.

Kwame Mensah

Founder of Code4Change, a nonprofit driving tech education in underserved communities. His 2021 talk on “Tech for Social Impact” inspired dozens of startups across West Africa.

Fatima Diallo

Cybersecurity expert and author. Her 2023 session on “Digital Defense in a Connected World” drew record attendance and led to new regional partnerships.
